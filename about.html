About

Hello! My name is Towe, and this is a website for my dog Cozmo.

I have created this website to hone my front-end developing skills. It's still a work in progress, as I am both learning and re-learning things at the same time.

I currently have a full-time day job that takes up most of my time during the day, and when I come home after work, I am usually drained of energy. So, progress is taking a while, but I am slowly adding more and more things.

I am currently adapting this website to mobile view, and later to tablet view as well. Future projects involve SVG canvas maps, forms, the usage of localstorage as well as more games.
